Egyptian Cuisine

Discover the rich flavors and traditional dishes of Egypt

Egyptian cuisine is a delightful mix of flavors, spices, and traditions. From hearty street food to elaborate feasts, Egyptian food is sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Popular Egyptian Dishes

Koshari

A comforting dish made of rice, lentils, pasta, and topped with a spicy tomato sauce and crispy fried onions.

Ful Medames

A traditional breakfast dish made from fava beans, served with olive oil, garlic, and lemon.

Mahshi

Vegetables like zucchini, eggplant, or grape leaves stuffed with rice and herbs, sometimes including meat.

Molokhia

A green leafy vegetable cooked into a soup, often served with rice and chicken or rabbit.

Hawawshi

A delicious meat-stuffed pita bread, baked or grilled to perfection.
